## Strecke 6 — Course detail (Buchlern → Hönggerberg)

**Route:** Buchlern → Hönggerberg | **10.4km / 234hm**
**Map/GPX:** https://trackmaxx.ch/maps/?m=ec368d93-aff4-4a7e-b0a5-24b7f9683a32&view=strecke6

### Key facts
- **Start:** Friedhofstrasse 89, 8048 Zürich (47.38083, 8.47716) — same physical location as Strecke 3
- **Jalons: RED** — ignore yellow jalons on course (those belong to Strecke 2 going the other direction)
- **Zeitneutralisation on Zürcherstrasse:** timer automatically pauses when entering the marked zone (red on SOLA-Map), resumes on exit — safety measure for traffic.
- **Waiting runners** in 100er-Sektoren
- **Changing rooms/showers:** Sportanlage Buchlern (at start)
- **Medical:** Sanitätszimmer in changing room building
- **Clothing transport:** Buchlern → Hönggerberg 10:10–12:00 (drop before start)
- **Catering:** Restaurant in changing room building, open from 07:30

### Character
> *Demanding — long leg with lots of ascent and descent; mix of natural roads and city passages on tarmac; almost exclusively in open terrain on tarmac*

Key implications:
- Unlike legs 3/5 (forest, technical), Strecke 6 is mostly **tarmac / open terrain** → road running shoes are appropriate (no trail spikes needed)
- Long leg at 10.4km → pacing discipline critical; not a sprint effort
- 234hm net gain across 10.4km = **22.5m/km average gradient** (moderate but sustained)
- Mix of uphill and downhill — eccentric load on quads on descents

Start elevation: 446m. Finish: 537m. Net gain: +91m. Total: 271hm (GPS-measured 2018).

### Neutral Zone — Tactical Use

The GPS shows a ~7-second involuntary stop at km 3.83 (traffic management point on Zürcherstrasse). The full neutralised zone spans the flat tarmac section approximately km 3.7–5.5. **The timer does not run while you are inside.**

Key principle: use the neutral zone for active recovery — drop to easy jog/walk, let HR drop, take nutrition, shake out arms — then rebuild to race pace over 1km before the monster climb. Entering the monster climb at HR ~165 vs ~188 saves an estimated 50+ seconds on that segment alone.

**Shoes:** Road trainers (not trail) — tarmac-dominant outside of the steep descent/climb path sections.

## General race notes

### Streckenmarkierung (all legs)
- Yellow jalons = standard legs
- **Red jalons = Strecken 3 and 6** (crossing routes — colour prevents confusion)
- No escorting runners on course (no bike, running escort, dog, etc.) → disqualification risk

### Zeitneutralisation (Strecken 2 and 6 only)
- Applies to section on Zürcherstrasse
- Timer pauses in marked zone (shown in red on SOLA-Map), resumes on exit
- Not a free rest — still running, but time doesn't count

### Mass starts / hunt starts
- Strecke 5: small mass start ~10:40 if not yet relayed
- Strecke 11: small mass start ~15:30
- Strecke 13: hunt start (Jagdstart) 16:30, based on aggregate time gaps
