## Summary

Magnesium — Supplementation Guide for Endurance Athletes ## Summary Magnesium is an essential mineral involved in >300 enzymatic reactions, ATP metabolism, muscle contraction, nerve transmission, and blood pressure regulation. Deficiency is common in athletes due to sweat losses and inadequate dietary intake. Oral supplementation is effective and well-evidenced.
