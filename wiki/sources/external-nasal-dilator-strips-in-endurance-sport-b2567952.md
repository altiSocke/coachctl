## Summary

External Nasal Dilator Strips in Endurance Sport Researched May 2026 | 9 sources incl. 1 meta-analysis | Overall confidence: High ## Summary External nasal dilator strips (Breathe Right® and equivalents) mechanically widen the nasal valve and measurably reduce nasal airway resistance at rest. Across 30 years of controlled trials, the consistent finding is that strips provide no meaningful benefit for VO₂max, power, running economy, or lactate threshold in healthy athletes with normal nasal anatomy.
