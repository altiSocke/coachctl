## Race Overview

The Tour de l'Uetliberg (TdU) is an annual Strava-segment timed trail race series based on the Uetliberg hill, Zurich. Not a mass-start race — athletes run each segment independently within a defined time window; Strava auto-records the time. Results aggregate into an overall TdU ranking.

- **Website:** https://www.tour-uetli.ch
- **Format:** 3 stages per edition, each with its own time window (typically 2–3 weeks)
- **Terrain:** 80–90% natural trail / forest path, some gravel and asphalt
- **Organized by:** Community-run, sponsored by LaceUp and Bächli Bergsport
- **Finisher stickers** collected near Hexenhüsli (Hot Roger stage), ~50m above finish

## TdU 9 (2026) — Stage Details

### Stage 1 — Güggel
- **Distance:** 3.7 km · **Elevation:** 276m ↑
- **Window:** Apr 10–26, 2026
- **Start:** Tramstation Albisgüetli (above, near Sportschützenhaus Aussersihl)
- **Finish:** End of Panoramaweg (near Albisgüetli)
- **Profile:** Mixed — climb via Föhreneggweg zigzag + metal stairs, ridge traverse, steep descent via Denzlerweg steps, flat return on Panoramaweg
- **Character:** 90% trail, 10% asphalt
- **Key hazards:** Metal stairs require full concentration; Denzlerweg steps are steep and quad-heavy on the way down

### Stage 2 — Detour
- **Distance:** 3.1 km · **Elevation:** 148m ↑ (descent first, then climb)
- **Window:** Apr 17–May 3, 2026
- **Start/Finish:** Aussichtsturm Uetliberg (summit observation tower)
- **Profile:** Down first via broad path + steep MTB trail (~100m descent), traverse south slope (Coiffeurweg) toward Stallikon, steep gravel climb back to Gratweg ridge, Uto Staffel, trail traverse, final Kanzel scramble (chains + railings), sprint finish to tower
- **Character:** 80% trail, 20% gravel
- **Key hazards:**
  - MTB-popular descent trail — watch for bikers
  - Final Kanzel climb uses fixed chains and railings; squeeze through railing onto platform
  - Slippery roots throughout
- **Strava route:** https://www.strava.com/routes/3361020228931568334

### Stage 3 — Hot Roger
- **Distance:** 3.2 km · **Elevation:** 390m ↑ (hardest stage)
- **Window:** Apr 24–May 10, 2026
- **Start:** Friesenbergstrasse/Schweighofstrasse junction (city level)
- **Finish:** Hohenstein playground, next to fountain (different from start)
- **Profile:** Asphalt warm-up 500m, Linderweg zigzag stairs, narrow technical ridge path to summit (hands needed — roots, fallen trees, round boulder = navigation marker), short plateau near TV tower, Gratweg descent via stairs and bridges to broad gravel path, Hohenstein
- **Character:** 80% trail, 10% gravel, 10% asphalt
- **Key hazards:**
  - Ridge path: wet roots, fallen tree debris, steep — hands required
  - Large round boulder mid-path = confirmation you're on route
  - Start and finish at different locations — plan transport accordingly
- **Finisher sticker:** ~50m above finish near large tree below road (exact location posted in race News)
- **Strava route:** https://www.strava.com/routes/3361019246019528174

## Shoe Recommendation

Trail shoes mandatory for all stages. Road/plated shoes are inappropriate:
- Loose MTB trail on Detour descent
- Wet roots and chains on Kanzel (Detour) and ridge path (Hot Roger)
- Steep compacted gravel (all stages)
