# Greifenseelauf — Half Marathon

Source: greifenseelauf.ch — captured 2026-04-24

## Event facts

- **Full name:** Greifenseelauf, Uster (also called Halbmarathon Greifensee)
- **2026 edition:** 47. Greifenseelauf
- **Date:** Saturday, 19 September 2026
- **Location:** Uster, Canton Zurich, Switzerland
- **Organiser:** Markus Ryffel's AG, Worb

## Distances

- Half Marathon: 21.1 km (main event)
- 10 km
- 6.3 km
- Kids races (Minigreifenseelauf U8–U14, FAMIGROS RUN & WIN)

## Course

- Runs through the Greifensee nature reserve
- **Surface:** ~60% asphalt, ~40% natural roads / gravel
- **Start:** Seestrasse, Uster (~10 min walk from Village/finish area)
- **Finish:** Stadtpark Uster (net chip timing — clock starts when you cross start line)
- **Exception:** Top 10 Overall finishers timed by gun time

## Pacemakers

Available for HM finish times: **1:30, 1:45, 2:00, 2:15** (and intermediate times).
Pacemakers carry a flag rucksack with target time printed.

## Start seeding

Athletes seeded by best time from last 3 years. First-time entrants seeded by self-declared time.

## Logistics

- Startnumber pickup: Stadthalle Uster (or postal delivery option in week before race)
- Bag drop / valuables: Stadthalle (envelope + coupon system)
- Changing rooms + showers: Berufsbildungszentrum Uster (on route to start)
- 11 medical posts along course + ambulance + doctor on course
- Public transport: SBB SwissRunners ticket (code on registration confirmation)
- Registration: trackmaxx.ch (race code GSL26)
